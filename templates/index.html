📖 What Citation Maven Does Citation Maven creates source notes by combining your citations with lead-in text—the words that appear just before each endnote reference in your document. This makes it easy to locate exactly where each note belongs. By default, your original citation format is preserved. You can optionally choose to reformat citations to a specific style guide.
💡 Pro Tip: Update Page Numbers After opening your converted document in Word:
  1. Press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select all
  2. Press F9 to update all page number fields
  3. Page numbers will automatically adjust when you edit the document!
